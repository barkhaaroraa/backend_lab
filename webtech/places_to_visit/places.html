The Digital Revolution: How Computers Transformed Our World

Computers have become an indispensable part of modern life, fundamentally changing how we work, communicate, learn, and entertain ourselves. From the early days of massive mainframes to the powerful devices we carry in our pockets, the evolution of computers has been incredible.

At the heart of every computer lies the central processing unit (CPU), the brain of the system that executes instructions and performs calculations. CPUs have become exponentially more powerful over time, thanks to advancements in technology and the relentless pursuit of performance by companies like Intel, AMD, and ARM.
